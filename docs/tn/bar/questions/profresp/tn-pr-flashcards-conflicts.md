**Q:** In TN, when must a contingency fee agreement be in writing?
**A:** Always; signed by the client; must state method of fee calculation and expenses allocation. (RPC 1.5(c))

**Q:** When is screening sufficient to avoid disqualification in TN after a lateral move?
**A:** If the lawyer is timely screened from participation, no fee is apportioned, and written notice is given; confirm RPC and case law for particulars. (RPC 1.10)

**Q:** What must a lawyer obtain before a business transaction with a client?
**A:** Written informed consent after full disclosure and the opportunity to seek independent counsel; terms must be fair and reasonable. (RPC 1.8(a))
