## Practitioner summary
The Office of Open Records Counsel’s **Best Practices & Guidelines** give statewide, nonbinding guidance for how entities should implement the Tennessee Public Records Act (TPRA). Core themes: **prompt access**, clear **PRRC/records-custodian roles**, reasonable **fees** (aligned with OORC’s Schedule), and using **economical, electronic** delivery when practicable. It also covers **citizenship/ID verification**, **segmented productions**, **mailing rules**, **redaction (incl. metadata)**, and **website posting** of policies and PRRC contact info. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

## Key points
- **Balance & no hindrance:** Policies (including fees) must not be used to **discourage or impede** access; records are **presumed open**. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}  
- **Required policy & PRRC:** Each entity must adopt a public records policy and identify a **Public Records Request Coordinator (PRRC)** and records custodians. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}  
- **Prompt response:** “Prompt” access is required; it is **not** prompt to **routinely wait seven business days** to respond. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}  
- **Citizenship & ID:** Only TN citizens have an enforceable right; entities may require **government photo ID** (or approved alternatives) and should state the rule in policy. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}  
- **Fees:** Consider budgets/resources; may **waive/reduce** in the public interest; use OORC **Schedule of Reasonable Charges** (presumption of reasonableness); document higher “actual cost.” :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}  
- **Labor choices:** Use staff who minimize **total cost** (sometimes a higher-wage, faster staffer is cheaper overall). :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}  
- **Process & forms:** When not practicable to promptly produce, respond using the **OORC Public Records Request Response Form**; provide denials **in writing**. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}  
- **Segmentation & vendors:** For large/long requests, **segment** production; if internal copying isn’t possible, use **prequalified vendors** and provide estimates/advance payment. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}  
- **Mailing:** If postage and copy costs are prepaid, mail via **USPS First-Class** to the requester’s home address (other methods may be used when feasible). :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}  
- **Custodian practices:** Provide a **supervised inspection space**; respond **economically**; produce **electronic copies** when feasible; keep copies of frequently requested/redacted records and post routine items online. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}  
- **Redaction:** Keep confidential info confidential while preserving record integrity; avoid redacting **originals**; beware **metadata** in electronic files; scan paper with mark-outs to PDF. :contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}  
- **Website:** Post the **public records policy** and **PRRC contact** on the homepage; post records with broad public interest (e.g., agendas, minutes) when practicable. :contentReference[oaicite:12]{index=12}
