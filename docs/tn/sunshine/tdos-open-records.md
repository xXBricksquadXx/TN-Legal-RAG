## Practitioner summary
The Tennessee Department of Safety & Homeland Security (TDOSHS) accepts Tennessee Public Records Act (TPRA) requests through its **Open Records portal**. Requesters should submit via the portal; fees apply under the Office of Open Records Counsel’s Schedule of Reasonable Charges and TDOS policy. Electronic delivery is preferred when practicable.

## Key points
- **Submit via TDOS Open Records portal** (linked above).
- Fees follow **OORC Schedule of Reasonable Charges** and TDOS policy.
- Use **electronic delivery** where feasible to reduce costs.

## Q&A (anchor)
**Q:** Where do I submit a TPRA request to the Department of Safety?  
**A:** Through the **TDOS Open Records portal** linked on the TDOS Open Records page; fees apply per OORC guidance.
