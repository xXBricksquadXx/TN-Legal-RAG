## Practitioner summary
The Tennessee Department of Safety & Homeland Security (TDOSHS) accepts TPRA requests by **email, mail, or in person** using agency forms. **Tennessee citizenship proof is required** for copies. Requests to inspect or copy **law-enforcement personnel files** must use **SF-1079**, and TDOSHS must **notify the officer within 3 business days** of any inspection/production. The process is governed by **TDOSHS Rule 1340-08-01** under **T.C.A. § 10-7-503(g)**.

## Key points
- **Where to send:** Email **Safety.OpenRecords@tn.gov** or deliver/mail to:  
  **312 Rosa L. Parks Ave., 25th Floor, Nashville, TN 37243**; or **1150 Foster Ave., Nashville, TN 37243**.
- **Forms:** **SF-1565 (Public Records Request / copies)** and **SF-1079 (Personnel File Inspection/Copies)**. TDOSHS also uses a Public Records Response template.
- **Citizenship proof (for copies):** Submit SF-1565 **with proof of Tennessee citizenship** (TN DL/ID, handgun permit, or other gov’t photo ID with address).
- **How to request inspection:** Call **615-251-5166**, appear **in person**, or submit **SF-1565**.
- **Personnel files:** **SF-1079** required; within **3 business days** TDOSHS notifies the officer of the requester and date.

## Notes / scope
- Fees/labor follow **OORC Schedule of Reasonable Charges** (T.C.A. § 8-4-604) as incorporated by agency rule.
- Check the **Secretary of State** site for the official text of Rule **1340-08-01**.

### Q&A (anchor)
**Q:** Where do I submit a TPRA request to the Department of Safety?  
**A:** Use the TDOS Open Records contact on this page — email **Safety.OpenRecords@tn.gov** or the listed Nashville addresses; fees follow OORC.
