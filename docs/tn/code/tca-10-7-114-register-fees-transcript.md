## Practitioner summary

For making transcripts, collation, and indexing under this part, the register is entitled to a statutory fee:

- one dollar ($1.00) per one hundred (100) words,

to be paid by the county.

## Key practice points

- The fee is calculated per 100 words of transcript work (including collation and indexing).
- The county is responsible for paying these fees.
- This is the core citation when calculating or justifying county payment obligations for transcript work.
