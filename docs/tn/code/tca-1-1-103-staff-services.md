## Practitioner summary

This section assigns staffing responsibility for the Tennessee code commission.
All staff support comes from the office of legal services for the general assembly.
The commission itself does not hire its own separate staff.

## Key practice points

- The office of legal services is the exclusive provider of staff services.
- When you need to contact “staff” for the commission, you’re really dealing with legislative legal services.
