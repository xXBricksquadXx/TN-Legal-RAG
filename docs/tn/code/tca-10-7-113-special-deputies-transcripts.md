## Practitioner summary

To ensure transcript work is completed promptly, this section authorizes the register to appoint as many deputies as are necessary to complete the transcripts as soon as practicable.

## Key practice points

- The register has express authority to scale up staff for transcription work.
- The standard is completion “at the earliest time practicable.”
- Useful when defending or planning staffing around large transcription/preservation projects.
